About Us

Experience quality learning with EdSpecial!

EdSpecial is an online learning platform that provides virtual one-on-one and personalized tutorials for students. We offer professional opportunities for Filipino tutors nationwide.

Our Mission

EdSpecial aims to achieve this vision by integrating technology to the teaching-learning process of students, equipping parents with what they need to catalyze educational transformation, and providing additional income to hundreds of Filipino teachers.

Our Vision

EdSpecial envisions to provide 21st-century quality education to Filipino learners while increasing the quality of life of the Filipino teachers.
